We could give you a ten page long Terms of Use. Like you'd read that. As far as we're concerned, it's simple...
  1. Use Freegle to pass on unwanted items direct to others.
  2. Everything must be free and legal.
  3. Be nice to other freeglers, and they'll be nice to you.
  4. We don't see or check items offered - see our disclaimer.
  5. Freegle communities may have additional rules - please check the welcome mail you get when you join for details.
  6. Please keep yourself safe and watch out for scams.
  7. Objectionable content or behaviour will not be tolerated.
  8. If you see anything inappropriate, suspicious or just dodgy then please report it.
  9. You must be aged 12 or over, as there is user-generated content.
  10. We won't sell your email address to anyone else - see privacy page.
  11. We will email you posts, replies, events, newsletters, etc - control these in Settings.
  12. We will make your post public to publicise Freegle but not your email address - see privacy page.
Happy freegling!

How to post an OFFER
  1. Go to https://www.ilovefreegle.org/give
  2. Click the 'Add photos' button to add a photo if you have one.
  3. Fill in the appropriate info in the various boxes.
  4. Click 'Next'.
  5. Put your postcode into the box.
  6. You will see, lower down the screen, a suggestion of which community to post on. If this is incorrect, click on the arrows and it will bring up a list of communities you are a member of.
  7. Click on 'Next'.
  8. Fill in your email address.
  9. Click 'Freegle it!'

How to join a community
  1. Go to https://www.ilovefreegle.org/explore
  2. Click on one of the 'regions' to see a list of communities in that area.
  3. Click on the 'Join' button, or click on 'Explore' to see what's being freegled.
  4. Alternatively, use the map to browse around, and either click on the 'Join' buttons below the map or reply to a post you're interested in.
